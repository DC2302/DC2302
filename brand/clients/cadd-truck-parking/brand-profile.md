## Identity

- **Brand name:** CADD Truck Parking
- **What we do (one sentence):** Safe and secure truck parking in the Permian Basin — the only lot in the Permian with our amenities.
- **Website / link for CTAs:** https://caddtruckparking.com/
- **Logo:** `brand/clients/cadd-truck-parking/assets/logo.png` — red "CADD" + gray script "Truck Parking" inside a semi-truck silhouette, transparent background (end-card ready)

## Voice & tone

- **Tone:** trusting and safe — a warm welcome from people who get truckers; brotherhood, peace of mind, rest
- **Words we use:** safe, secure, clean, organized, big rigs, tractor trailer, best value, best bang for the buck, only lot in the Permian with our amenities
- **Words we never use:** cheap, unsafe, expensive
- **Bilingual:** Spanish welcome is part of the brand — standing sign-off: "y no te olvides que también hablamos español"

## Visual style (the "style lock")
