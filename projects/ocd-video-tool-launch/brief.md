# Creative Brief: "Your Business, On Video — Without Lifting a Camera" (OCD launch)

- **Goal:** lead-generation — the product is a new done-for-you service sold through OSO's consulting relationship; the win is a booked call, not a click-through sale or vague awareness. (Intake said agents-decide.)
- **Audience:** Small/medium business owners — the same efficiency-minded operators OSO already consults for — who know they need video on social media but have no time, no team, and no editing skills, so their brand stays invisible while competitors post daily.
- **Core promise:** OSO Content Design turns your brand and one idea into finished, brand-consistent marketing videos — produced end-to-end by an AI-agent pipeline, so you get a consistent video presence with zero hours of your time.
- **Hook (first 2 seconds):**
  1. **[STRONGEST]** "This entire video was made by the tool it's selling." — self-referential proof; the video itself is the demo, an instant pattern-interrupt that earns the next 38 seconds and pre-answers the "is it any good?" objection.
  2. "You don't have a video problem. You have a time problem." — reframe hook; speaks directly to the optimization-minded owner in OSO's voice, but is quieter on the scroll.
  3. "Your competitors post video every day. You post... nothing." — pain/contrast hook; strong stopping power but risks a negative open that's slightly off OSO's confident, solution-forward tone.
- **Message arc:** (4 beats = 4 blocks x 10s)
  1. **Hook / proof-of-concept** — "This entire video was made by the tool it's selling." Grizzly badge world, electric-blue energy; plant the meta-claim.
  2. **The pain, reframed** — You know video works, but filming, editing, and staying on-brand is a full-time job you don't have. That's an efficiency problem — and efficiency is what OSO does.
  3. **The solution** — Introducing OSO Content Design: send us your brand and one idea; an agentic AI pipeline scripts, designs, voices, and edits a finished, obsessively on-brand video — ads, lead-gen, brand films. Every video matches your brand, every time.
  4. **Payoff + CTA** — Smarter workflows. Stronger bottom line. Now for your content too. Book a 30-minute consultation → osoptimization.com/en. End on the OSO-OCD shield logo.
- **CTA:** "Book a 30-minute consultation" (link: https://www.osoptimization.com/en) — reusing the user's standing consultation CTA from the prior submission; recommend writing it back to the brand profile as the standing lead-generation CTA.
- **Platforms:** Primary: Instagram Reels, 9:16 (matches brand-profile default aspect; where SMB owners actually scroll). Secondary: LinkedIn (B2B buyer intent, same 9:16 cut) and TikTok / YouTube Shorts (free redistribution of the same asset).
- **Length:** 40 seconds (4 blocks x 10s) — first production run; long enough for problem → solution → proof → CTA without padding, inside the 30s–2min effectiveness sweet spot.
- **Tone notes:** Confident, declarative, zero hedging — an optimization consultant who has already solved this, not a startup begging for attention. Efficiency language over creative-agency language ("workflow," "pipeline," "output," "bottom line"). Visuals locked to the style key: ink-navy/steel environments, chrome detail, electric ice-blue glow, esports-badge energy with the grizzly mascot. Weave in the tagline "Smarter Workflows. Stronger Bottom Line." at the payoff. Confident, never smug; premium, never hype.
- **Proof points / facts used:**
  - 82% of marketers report good ROI from video marketing — [Wyzowl, Video Marketing Statistics 2026](https://wyzowl.com/video-marketing-statistics/)
  - 63% of consumers say a short video is how they'd most like to learn about a product or service — [Wyzowl 2026](https://wyzowl.com/video-marketing-statistics/)
  - Lack of time and lack of in-house filming/editing skills are the top cited barriers keeping marketers/businesses from doing video — [Wyzowl 2026](https://wyzowl.com/video-marketing-statistics/), [Digital Applied, Video Marketing Statistics 2026](https://www.digitalapplied.com/blog/video-marketing-statistics-2026-data-points)
  - Structural proof: the video itself is OCD output — the strongest claim needs no statistic.
- **Risks:**
  - AI-video fatigue: "AI-generated content" can read as low-quality slop to skeptical owners. Mitigation: lead with the finished result (the video itself) and brand consistency, not the word "AI"; say "agentic pipeline" once, show quality throughout.
  - Overclaim risk: avoid "in minutes," "unlimited videos," or guaranteed-results language — none is verified. Stick to "finished, on-brand videos without your time."
  - Two-brand confusion: OSO (consulting) vs. OCD (content service). Keep OSO as the trusted parent, OCD as the offering; one logo lockup at the end, not competing identities.
  - Statistics on screen can feel like a webinar; if used, limit to one stat maximum (82% ROI) as a supporting caption in beat 2, never as the hook.
  - The self-referential hook must be true — every frame of this video must actually come from the pipeline, or the claim collapses.
