## Annual plan (Elite status)

Paying annually gets a discounted price equivalent to two free months, plus
a personalized (reserved) parking space.

## Payment

We accept card payments by secure payment link (Square) sent by text
message during the call, plus Zelle, Cash App, and ACH through the payment
page on our website. We never take card numbers over the phone.

## Availability

Availability changes frequently. If the caller wants a space, collect their
name and number and book a callback so management can confirm current
availability, or direct them to the reservation page at caddtruckparking.com.
