# Frequently asked questions

**Do you offer emergency service?**
Yes, 24/7. The emergency call-out fee is $95.

**Are you licensed and insured?**
Yes — licensed, bonded, and insured in the state.

**How do I book an appointment?**
The assistant can take the caller's name, phone number, address, and a short
description of the problem, and a team member will call back within one
business hour to confirm a time.

**Do you offer financing?**
Yes, on jobs over $1,000 through our financing partner.
